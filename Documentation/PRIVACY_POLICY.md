# SimFolio Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: April 4, 2026**

## Introduction

SimFolio ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our SimFolio mobile application (the "App").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

- **Profile Information**: School name, graduation year, and display name (optional)
- **Portfolio Data**: Procedure types, photo tags, tooth numbers, and portfolio configurations you create
- **Photo Metadata**: Tags, ratings, and organizational data you assign to photos (photos remain in your device's photo library)
- **Account Information**: Email address or Apple ID identifier used to create and authenticate your SimFolio account
- **Shared Photos**: Clinical procedure photos you choose to share with classmates through the social feed
- **Social Content**: Comments and reactions you post on shared photos

### Information Collected Automatically

When you use our App, we may automatically collect certain information, including:

- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Data**: Features used, screens viewed, actions taken within the App
- **Crash Data**: Technical information about app crashes and errors to help us improve stability
- **Performance Data**: App launch times, response times, and other performance metrics

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Provide, maintain, and improve the App
- Understand how users interact with the App
- Identify and fix bugs, crashes, and technical issues
- Process subscription purchases and manage your account
- Send you technical notices and support messages
- Comply with legal obligations

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

### Firebase Analytics (Google)

We use Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with our App. Firebase Analytics collects:

- App usage patterns and feature engagement
- Screen views and user flows
- Device information (model, OS version)
- Geographic region (country/region level)

Firebase Analytics data is aggregated and does not identify individual users. For more information, see [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

### Firebase Crashlytics (Google)

We use Firebase Crashlytics to collect crash reports and diagnostic information, including:

- Crash logs and stack traces
- Device state at time of crash
- Device model and OS version
- App version information

This data helps us identify and fix bugs to improve app stability. Crash data does not include personal information. For more information, see [Firebase Crashlytics Data Collection](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy).

### Firebase Authentication (Google)

We use Firebase Authentication to manage user accounts for the social features. Firebase Authentication collects:

- Email addresses or Apple ID identifiers used to sign in
- Account creation and last sign-in timestamps
- Unique user identifiers (UIDs) linked to your account

This information is used solely to authenticate you and maintain your account. For more information, see [Firebase Authentication Data Security](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy).

### Firebase Firestore (Google)

We use Firebase Firestore to store data that powers the social features. Firestore stores:

- **User profiles**: Display name and school affiliation
- **Posts**: Photos you choose to share, including captions and procedure metadata
- **Comments**: Text comments you write on shared posts
- **Reactions**: Emoji reactions you add to posts
- **Reports**: Content reports you submit for moderation
- **Block lists**: Records of users you have blocked

This data is necessary to provide the social feed and related features. For more information, see [Firebase Privacy and Security](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy).

### Firebase Storage (Google)

We use Firebase Storage to store photos you upload for sharing. This includes:

- Full-resolution photos you choose to share with classmates
- Compressed thumbnails generated for feed display

Photos stored in Firebase Storage are accessible to other verified users at your school. Photos you have not chosen to share remain solely on your device. For more information, see [Firebase Privacy and Security](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy).

### RevenueCat

We use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions and in-app purchases. RevenueCat collects:

- Purchase history and subscription status
- Anonymous user identifiers
- Device information for fraud prevention

RevenueCat does not have access to your payment information, which is handled securely by Apple. For more information, see [RevenueCat's Privacy Policy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy).

### Apple Services

- **App Store**: Subscription purchases are processed through Apple's App Store. Apple handles all payment information according to their [Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).
- **Photo Library**: Photos remain stored in your device's photo library. We only store metadata (tags, ratings) that you assign to photos.

## Data Shared with Classmates

When you participate in social features, certain information becomes visible to other verified users at your school:

- **Display name**: The name shown on your posts and comments
- **School affiliation**: Your school is used to determine which feed you can access
- **Shared photos**: Clinical procedure photos you explicitly choose to post
- **Comments**: Text comments you write on posts in the shared feed
- **Reactions**: Emoji reactions you add to posts

Only users verified at the same school can see this content. We do not share your email address, device information, or any other account details with other users.

## App Tracking Transparency

When you first use the App, we will ask for your permission to track your activity across other companies' apps and websites. This tracking is used to:

- Measure advertising effectiveness
- Provide aggregated analytics

You can change your tracking preferences at any time in your device's Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking.

## Your Privacy Choices

### Analytics Opt-Out

You can disable analytics and crash reporting at any time:

1. Open SimFolio
2. Go to Profile > Data Management
3. Toggle off "Analytics & Crash Reports"

When disabled, we will not collect usage data or crash reports from your device.

### Data Export

You can export your data at any time:

1. Go to Profile > Data Management
2. Tap "Export Metadata" or "Export Portfolios"

### Data Deletion

You can delete all your data stored by the App:

1. Go to Profile > Data Management
2. Tap "Reset App" to clear all local data

To request deletion of any data stored on our servers, contact us at the email address below.

## Data Storage and Security

- **Local Storage**: Portfolio data, photo metadata, and preferences are stored locally on your device
- **Cloud Services**: Analytics data is stored on Google's Firebase servers; subscription data is stored on RevenueCat's servers; social feature data (profiles, posts, comments, reactions, and uploaded photos) is stored on Google's Firebase servers
- **Security**: We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your data. Firebase Storage and Firestore access is restricted by security rules that prevent unauthorized access to your data.

## Data Retention

- **Analytics Data**: Retained for up to 14 months by Firebase Analytics
- **Crash Data**: Retained for up to 90 days by Firebase Crashlytics
- **Subscription Data**: Retained by RevenueCat for the duration of your subscription plus applicable legal retention periods
- **Local Data**: Retained on your device until you delete it or uninstall the App
- **Social Profile and Content**: User profiles, posts, comments, and reactions are retained until you delete your account
- **Uploaded Photos**: Photos stored in Firebase Storage are retained until you delete the post or delete your account

### Social Data Retention and Deletion

When you delete your SimFolio account, we permanently remove all associated social data within 24–48 hours, including:

- Your Firebase Authentication credentials
- Your Firestore user profile, posts, comments, reactions, reports, and block list entries
- All photos and thumbnails you uploaded to Firebase Storage

This includes any content you shared with classmates — all shared content is permanently and irreversibly deleted when your account is removed.

## Image Moderation

If automated image moderation is enabled, photos you upload for sharing may be screened by automated content moderation services before becoming visible to other users. This screening checks for content that violates our community guidelines. Moderation results are used solely to enforce our policies and are not used for advertising or shared with third parties.

## Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

## International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own, including the United States, where our service providers (Google, RevenueCat) maintain servers. These countries may have different data protection laws than your country of residence.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

- Posting the new Privacy Policy in the App
- Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

European residents have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation, including the rights listed above and the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.
