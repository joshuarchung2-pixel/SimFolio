## Description (4,000 characters max)

```
SimFolio is the essential portfolio management app designed specifically for dental students. Capture, organize, and track your clinical procedure photography with ease.

CAPTURE WITH CONFIDENCE
• Purpose-built camera for dental photography
• Tag photos by procedure, stage, and angle
• Tooth number tracking for precise documentation
• Quick capture workflow optimized for clinical settings

ORGANIZE YOUR WORK
• Create portfolios for different courses or requirements
• Filter and search by procedure type, stage, or tooth
• Rate your best work with 5-star ratings
• Add favorites for quick access to standout photos

TRACK YOUR PROGRESS
• Set requirements for each portfolio
• Visual progress tracking shows completion status
• Due date reminders keep you on schedule
• See which procedures still need documentation

PROFESSIONAL PHOTO EDITING
• Adjust brightness, contrast, and exposure
• Fine-tune highlights, shadows, and black point
• Enhance with saturation and sharpness controls
• Crop and straighten for perfect framing
• Non-destructive editing preserves originals

EXPORT & SHARE
• Export portfolios for submission
• Share individual photos or collections
• Background export for large portfolios
• Maintain image quality for assessment

BUILT FOR DENTAL STUDENTS
SimFolio understands the unique requirements of dental education. Whether you're documenting Class I restorations, crown preparations, or any other procedure, SimFolio helps you build a comprehensive portfolio that meets your program's standards.

PREMIUM FEATURES
Subscribe to unlock unlimited portfolios, advanced editing tools, and priority support. Free users can create one portfolio to get started.

Privacy focused: Your photos stay on your device. We don't access, collect, or share your clinical images.

Questions or feedback? We'd love to hear from you at support@simfolio.app.
```
