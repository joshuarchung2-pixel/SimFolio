## App Description (4000 characters max)

SimFolio is the essential portfolio management app designed specifically for dental students and professionals. Capture, organize, and track your clinical work with ease.

**Smart Capture**
- Tag photos before you capture them - set procedure type, stage, angle, and tooth number
- Batch capture mode for taking multiple photos with the same tags
- Rate your photos immediately after capture
- Grid overlay for perfect composition

**Portfolio Tracking**
- Create portfolios with specific requirements
- Track progress with visual indicators
- Set due dates and receive reminders
- See exactly which photos you still need

**Powerful Organization**
- Filter photos by procedure, stage, angle, or rating
- Search across all your clinical work
- View photos in grid or list format
- Favorite your best work for quick access

**Easy Export**
- Export portfolios as ZIP, PDF, or individual files
- Organize exports by procedure, date, or custom grouping
- Choose image quality settings
- Share directly to instructors or colleagues

**Built for Dental Education**
- Pre-configured procedure types (Class I-V, Crown, Bridge, Veneer, and more)
- Customizable procedure list
- Dental-specific tagging (tooth numbers, quadrants)
- Progress tracking aligned with clinical requirements

Download SimFolio today and take control of your dental portfolio.

## App Store Optimization Tips

### Title Optimization
- Keep the main title short and memorable
- Use the subtitle for keywords
- Avoid keyword stuffing

### Description Best Practices
- Lead with benefits, not features
- Use bullet points for readability
- Include a call to action
- Mention unique selling points early

### Screenshot Strategy
- Show the app in use, not just UI
- Add captions explaining features
- Use consistent design language
- Show variety of features across screenshots

### Keyword Selection
- Focus on relevant, searchable terms
- Avoid trademarked terms
- Use singular forms (they match plurals too)
- Research competitor keywords
